Overview

Purpose

The website contains everything you need to understand the grim dark world of Warhammer 40k. The website contains not only information of the world of Warhammer 40k but also details relating to the tabletop wargame of the same name.

Audience

For those who are interested in jumping into a grim dark sci-fi world of the far future.

Content

Home page

This will contain a key quote describing the universe of Warhammer 40K in general, a few basic descriptions of the of the three main groups, a small guide in terms of the tabletop game, and a small guide on model building and painting.

Images for the Home page

Imperium of Man

This page will go over the varying factions of the Imperium of Man, detailing what they are and what to expect from then on the Tabletop game. After which a few showcase models will be shown. Lastly a small bit of background history will be given for context purposes.

Images for the Page 2

Forces of Chaos

A brief history of this general faction will be shared for context purposes, factions in detail and of how they play will be given here, along with showcase models.

Images for the Page 3

Xenos

A brief history of each faction will be shared for context purposes, factions in detail of what they are and of how they play will be given here, along with showcase models. alongside this a few subfactions will also be shared as well and fun facts.
